TechCon History

TechCon was founded in 2005 by a group of passionate tech enthusiasts who envisioned a platform for sharing knowledge, fostering connections, and inspiring innovation within the tech industry. Over the years, TechCon has grown into a premier conference, attracting leading figures and attendees from around the world.

Mission

TechCon's mission is to empower tech professionals and enthusiasts by providing a platform for:

  • Learning from industry leaders through inspiring talks and workshops.
  • Discovering cutting-edge technologies and exploring their potential.
  • Connecting with fellow innovators and fostering a collaborative tech community.

Past Speakers

TechCon has been privileged to host some of the brightest minds in the tech industry. Here are a few notable speakers from previous years:

Elon Musk

Elon Musk (born June 28, 1971) is a South African-born American entrepreneur and business magnate known for his ambitious ventures in technology and space exploration. He is the founder, CEO, and chief engineer of SpaceX; angel investor, CEO, and product architect of Tesla, Inc.; co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI; and president of The Boring Company.

Fred Swaniker

Fred Swaniker (born 1976) is a Ghanaian serial entrepreneur and leadership development expert. He is passionate about transforming Africa through ethical and entrepreneurial leadership. Swaniker recognized the importance of leadership early on, having served as the headmaster of a secondary school founded by his mother at the young age of 17. This experience likely fueled his desire to address the leadership gap he saw in Africa.
